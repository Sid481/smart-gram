Introduction

Tembhurni is a progressive village committed to rural development, transparency, and citizen welfare through modern governance.

Mission

Ensure transparent governance, equitable development, and delivery of essential services to all citizens of our village.

Vision

A self-reliant, digitally empowered village with excellent infrastructure and quality services for everyone.

Sarpanch Message

Dedicated to serving our community with integrity and building a prosperous future together for all residents.

Village Profile

Tembhurni is a vibrant and progressive village located in Madha Taluka of Solapur District, Maharashtra. The Grampanchayat plays a pivotal role in local administration and development, working tirelessly to provide essential services, promote agricultural growth, and ensure the welfare of all citizens.
